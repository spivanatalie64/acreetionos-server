# Our Mission

**Technology should be free. Not just free as in price — free as in freedom.**

AcreetionOS exists because we believe everyone deserves access to the same
powerful computing experience that big corporations have. Not a stripped-down,
data-harvesting, ad-riddled version — the real thing.

## What Big Corporate Does

Every major operating system vendor has decided that your computer is not yours.

- **Microsoft** ships telemetry, ads, and AI copilot features you didn't ask for
  directly into Windows. Your data trains their models. Your desktop shows their ads.
- **Apple** locks you into a walled garden, controls what software you can run,
  and takes 30% of every transaction.
- **Google** builds ChromeOS and Android around surveillance capitalism. You are
  the product, not the customer.
- **Canonical** ships Amazon ads in the Unity dash (remember that?) and telemetry
  by default in Ubuntu.

They've convinced an entire generation that this is normal. It's not.

## What We Do Differently

**AcreetionOS is different because we don't want anything from you.**

- No telemetry. No data collection. No "phone home" services.
- No ads. No sponsored content. No promoted packages.
- No AI features that require cloud connectivity.
- No corporate agenda. No VC funding. No exit strategy.
- Just a libre, open-source operating system that respects you.

We use Arch Linux as our foundation because it embodies the Unix philosophy:
simplicity, transparency, and user control. But we've built on top of it to
create something that's Arch-based, just better, and more stable.

## What We Offer

- **A full desktop experience** — Cinnamon, polished and configured for
  productivity out of the box
- **Server-grade tooling** — The same stack you'd find in any data center,
  pre-installed and ready to go
- **Auto-installing ISOs** — Boot, wait, and you're done. No complex setup.
- **Quarterly LTS-based releases** — Stability you can depend on
- **Zero compromises** — No data collection, no tracking, no ads, no nonsense

## Why "Server with Desktop Experience"?

Because we believe system administrators deserve nice things too. The command
line is powerful, but sometimes you need a browser with documentation, a
terminal multiplexer with a GUI, or a monitoring dashboard on the big screen.

Windows Server has had Desktop Experience for years. We think Linux servers
deserve the same respect. The difference is: with us, you can strip it down
to TTY with a single command if you change your mind.

## The Bottom Line

We build AcreetionOS because we love building operating systems. We share it
because we believe software should be free. We maintain it because we use it
ourselves — on our workstations, our servers, our infrastructure.
